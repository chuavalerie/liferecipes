There’s something about the sky — its warmth, its light. A smile forms, a soft breath escapes. How wonderful it all feels.

“I never get tired of the blue sky.” - Vincent Van Gogh

A recipe for golden moments

It’s that golden hour, that rim of light that illuminates your face. Simmer in joy and remember you’re unapologetically alive.


Ingredients

  • Cap
  • Necklaces
  • Denim shorts
  • Lace and cottom
  • Sandals
  • Frilly dresses

Instructions

  1. Layer cotton and lace over sun-kissed skin — light and airy, dancing in the beams of golden light.
  2. Clip your hair up, slide on your sandals, and let your ankles meet the breeze.
  3. Top it off with a boyish cap, stack your necklaces, and carry your sense of wonder.
  4. Chase golden hours — with your eyes, not your phone — and bask in conversations that fill your heart with ease.
