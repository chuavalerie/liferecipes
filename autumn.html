A recipe for embracing change

YThat crisp air, the rustle of leaves, and the slow shift of green to red, to yellow — until they slip gently from the branch.


Ingredients

  • Scarves
  • Thin layering jackets
  • Warm neutrals
  • Warm tea
  • Bucket cap

Instructions

  1. Wrap a scarf gently around your neck, pair it with your cutest bucket cap.
  2. Layer on your jacket and do a twirl.
  3. Add a dash of warm, peachy blush — fitting for the blushing weather — to bring out that soft, sun-kissed glow.
  4. Take slow walks, listen to the crunch of leaves, and admire all that’s left… and all that’s still to come.
