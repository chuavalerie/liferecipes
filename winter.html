Winter☃

When everything fades to black and white, you start to see the grey. Winter may feel lonely, but you come to realise — you are all the comfort you need.


"The longer I live, the more beautiful life becomes." - Frank Lloyd Wright

A recipe for gratitude

You’re feeling tired and dreary, but then you notice greens, reds, and tiny buds sprouting. You look up—flowers, oh flowers, all around.


Ingredients

  • Trusty hydroflask
  • Cute onesie jumpsuit
  • Thermal layers
  • Beanie
  • Gloves
  • Waterproof boots
  • Wool socks
  • Winter jackets

Instructions

  1. Fold in candlelight, light up your fireplace and see the place shimmer in dim, cozy lights.
  2. Wear your onesies, your beanies, your wooly socks and wrap them closely.
  3. Grab your trusty hydroflask, or stanleys; take a sip of warmth that runs through your body.
  4. Reach out your hand and let that snowflake settle slowly.

Featured sights

I spy with my little eye the many nuances winter brings — the white, the dull, and everything in between. Have you ever stopped to notice what they might have once been? Their original shapes, their fading bodies, and holes that whisper of what was once so very green.
