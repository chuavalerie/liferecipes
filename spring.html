Spring☂

Setting aside Winter’s unfinished business and chilly temperament, Spring might be one of my favourite things.


"Every day I discover more and more beautiful things." - Claude Monet

A recipe for new beginnings

You’re feeling tired and dreary, but then you notice greens, reds, and tiny buds sprouting. You look up—flowers, oh flowers, all around.


Ingredients

  • Floral prints
  • A fellow cup (for coffee or tea)
  • Linen tops
  • Light cardigans
  • Pastel skirts

Instructions

  1. Pull the blinds and let the sunlight pour through open windows — fresh and fragrant.
  2. Let out a light sigh. Indulge in colour — both around you and within you.
  3. Dress yourself in blossoms: soft linen, pastel layers, and light cardigans.
  4. Pour a fellow cup, swirl the ice, and feel the gladness bloom from the inside out.
